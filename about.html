    • Who is Carrie?

    Carrie is a UX/UI Designer, focus in Information Architecture, User Experience and User Interface Design. She is well equipped with design knowledge and solid experience in the design industry. She has worked for agency, corporate and retail environments with experience working on B2B and B2C clients. She is a user-centred designer adopting the principles of UCD methodology from user research to define audiences and concept to execution to deliver the design or end product in Agile process. She is a highly motivated and ambitious individual who is adaptable to changes and makes the most of these in any field. You can know more about her Visually ortextually.
